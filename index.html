About Neatenize

Neatenize is a simple, easy-to-use checklist to help you reorganize, tidy up, and clean out. It’s loosely based on the Konmari method, which sorts items by category instead of room.

While it’s a very thorough list, it’s not exhaustive; not all categories will apply to your needs, and you may have other categories not on this list. Use Neatenize as a starting point and framework.

How to save checklist progress

Create an account to save your progress and access it on multiple devices.

Printing this checklist

Neatenize should print well enough as-is, but you may need to tweak margins in your print settings. Due to bugs in Chrome and other browsers, the checklist sections may break over pages.

When to keep what?

Important receipts

These include receipts for large purchases such as musical instruments, computers, cars, etc.

Keep them for as long as you still have that item.

Warranty cards & manuals

These include manuals and warranties for large purchases such as electronics, appliances, furniture, etc.

Keep them for as long as you still have that item.

Tax returns, tax documents, health insurance, property records & business paperwork

The length of time varies depending on the type of document. The IRS has guidelines for how long to keep each type of document.

Rule of thumb: keep them for at least 7 years.
